Description

Pizza is a savory dish consisting of a flat, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is typically baked in an oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Common toppings include pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, sausage, olives, and bell peppers. Variations exist, such as thin-crust, deep-dish, and stuffed-crust pizzas, offering diverse textures and flavors.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Vegtables

Steps

  1. Prepare dough
  2. Preheat oven
  3. Roll out dough
  4. Add sauce
  5. Add cheese
  6. Add toppings
  7. Bake
  8. Slice and serve
