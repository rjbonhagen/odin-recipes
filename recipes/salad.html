Description

Salad is a dish consisting of a mixture of fresh ingredients, typically vegetables, fruits, proteins, and dressings. Common base ingredients include leafy greens like lettuce, spinach, or kale, while toppings may include tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots, cheese, nuts, and croutons. Dressings such as vinaigrette, ranch, or Caesar add flavor. Salads can be served as a side dish or a main course with proteins like grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu.

Ingredients

  • Base Greens
  • Vegtables
  • Protein
  • Cheese
  • Crunchy toppings
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. Wash and prepare ingredients
  2. Assemble the base
  3. Add vegetables
  4. Add protein
  5. Sprinkle cheese and toppings
  6. Drizzle Dressing
  7. Toss and serve
